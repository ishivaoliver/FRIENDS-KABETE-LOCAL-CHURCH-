About Our Church

Welcome to Friends Church Quakers Kabete, a community grounded in faith, love, and service. For over 40 years, we have been dedicated to fostering spiritual growth, sharing the Gospel, and making a positive impact in our community.

Our Mission

To glorify God by loving Him, serving others, and reaching the world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To build a diverse, inclusive, and Christ-centered community where everyone feels welcomed, valued, and empowered to live a life of purpose and faith.
