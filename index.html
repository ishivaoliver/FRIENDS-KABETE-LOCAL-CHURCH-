About Our Church

Welcome to Friends Church Quakers Kabete, a community grounded in faith, love, and service. For over 40 years, we have been dedicated to fostering spiritual growth, sharing the Gospel, and making a positive impact in our community.

Our Mission

To preach and teach the Gospel of God's love and salvation through Jesus Christ to all mankind.

Our Vision

A well-resourced, gospel-preaching, and Christ-centered church.
